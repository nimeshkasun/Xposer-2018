Xposer 2018

 What is Xposer?

Xposer is an event, which is organized by second year students of KDU – Southern Campus in every year.

It may organized either as a day function or night function. It is colored with many fun games together with professional DJ.

Vision

It is organized as a welcome for the freshers of KDU and annual get together of Day Scholars of Southern Campus.

 Xposer 2018

In this year it is going to be a huge event with full of surprises and lot of fun games. Xposer – 2018 is organized by Intake 34 students of KDU – Southern Campus.

It’s The Best Time!
This event will earn you a chance to know each other and improve your friendship.
